Momo is the creature of all creatures

Momo is a sleek black cat with curious golden eyes, rescued as a tiny kitten from a bustling construction site. Despite his rough start, he quickly grew into a playful and affectionate companion, always seeking the warmest spot to nap or a new adventure to chase. His favorite pastime is perching by the window, watching the world outside with quiet fascination.
